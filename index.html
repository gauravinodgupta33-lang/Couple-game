How to Play

  • Tap the dice to roll and move your token forward.
  • Landing on a square pops up a task — Accept it or Skip it.
  • Skipping doesn't end your turn — roll again and move onward from where you are.
  • Accept 3 tasks to earn 1 skip charge for later.
  • First to reach square 200 wins!
